What's inside

Built for the
working broadcaster

Every feature in RadioDesk exists because a working radio host needed it. No bloat, no learning curve — just the tools that matter.

01

DAW-Style Crossfade & Segue Editor

Set in and out points visually, control duck depth, and preview transitions before your show. The same smooth handoff as a professional broadcast console.

02

Waveform Display with Playhead

See your audio, not just hear it. Visual waveform display so you always know exactly where you are in a track. Click to scrub to any point.

03

Multi-Track Audio Management

Cue, organize, and fire tracks without the chaos. Drag to reorder, search by title or artist, and keep your show tight from start to finish.

04

Broadcast Cue Sheet Export

Generate a broadcast-standard CSV log with offset timestamps, media type, title, artist, album, and year. Most logging systems import this format directly.

05

Local Artist Tagging

Flag and track local music in your rotation. Built for hosts who take community programming seriously and need to report local content accurately.

06

Native Mac App · No Subscription

One-time purchase. No account required, no cloud dependency, no monthly fee. RadioDesk lives on your machine and works offline, always.
